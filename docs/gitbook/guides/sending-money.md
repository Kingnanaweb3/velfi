# Sending money

The simplest thing you can do on Velfi: send money to someone instantly.

### How to send

Just tell Velfi what you want:

> "Send $50 to jordan.vel."

Velfi shows you the payment — who it's going to and how much — and asks you to confirm. Tap to approve, and it's done. The money arrives in under a second.

### Who you can send to

* **A `.vel` name** — like `jordan.vel`. The easiest way.
* **An email address** — if the person isn't on Velfi yet. They'll get an invite to claim it. See [Send to an email](email-to-claim.md).

### Things to know

* You always see exactly what you're sending and to whom before it goes out.
* Payments between `.vel` names settle instantly and cost effectively nothing.
* Once confirmed, a payment is recorded on-chain and can't be reversed — so check the name before you approve.

{% hint style="info" %}
Not sure you typed the right name? Velfi shows you the recipient before you confirm. If it doesn't look right, cancel and try again.
{% endhint %}
