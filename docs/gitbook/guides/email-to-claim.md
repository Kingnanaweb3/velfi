# Send to an email

You can send money to someone who isn't on Velfi yet. They get an invitation, sign up, and claim it.

### How it works

> "Send $50 to jordan@gmail.com — they claim it when they sign up."

1. You send the payment to an email address.
2. The recipient gets an invitation to claim it.
3. They sign in with Google, claim a `.vel` name, and the money lands in their new account.

### Why it's useful

* **Pay anyone, even first-timers.** The recipient doesn't need to already have Velfi or know anything about crypto.
* **Onboarding is built in.** Receiving money is the reason they join — the easiest possible first experience.

{% hint style="info" %}
Until the recipient claims it, the funds are held safely. If they never claim it, you can reclaim it.
{% endhint %}

### Common uses

* Sending money to family or friends who aren't on Velfi yet.
* Paying someone new without asking them to set anything up first.
* Inviting people to Velfi in the most natural way — by paying them.
