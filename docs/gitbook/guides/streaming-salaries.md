# Streaming salaries

Streaming pays someone continuously over time, instead of in one lump sum. It's ideal for salaries, contractor pay, and vesting.

### How to stream

> "Stream $4,000 to alex.vel over 30 days."

Velfi sets up a stream that releases the money gradually across the period you specify. The recipient can access what's been streamed so far at any point.

### Why stream instead of send?

* **For the payer:** you commit the full amount up front, but it's paid out steadily — aligned with work being done over time.
* **For the recipient:** they earn continuously rather than waiting for a single payday.

### Common uses

* Paying a monthly salary that accrues daily.
* Contractor agreements paid over the length of a project.
* Vesting funds to a partner or team member over time.

### Managing a stream

You can see the progress of any stream you've set up — how much has been released and how much remains. Streams run automatically once you've approved them.
