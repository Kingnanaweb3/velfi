# Splitting payments

Need to divide a payment between several people? Just describe the split and Velfi handles the math.

### How to split

> "Split this 40/30/30 between alice.vel, bob.vel, and carol.vel."

Velfi works out each person's share, shows you the breakdown, and asks you to confirm. One approval sends every portion at once.

### Ways to split

* **By percentage** — "split 50/50," "split 40/30/30."
* **By equal shares** — "split this evenly between four people."
* **By exact amounts** — "send $100 to alice.vel and $50 to bob.vel."

### Common uses

* Splitting rent between roommates.
* Dividing a group dinner or trip cost.
* Paying out multiple collaborators from one payment.
* Distributing revenue between partners.

{% hint style="success" %}
Everyone in the split is paid at the same time, in one action. No chasing people for their share afterward.
{% endhint %}
