# How it works

Velfi turns a sentence into a payment. Here's what happens between you typing an instruction and the money moving.

### The four steps

1. **You write an instruction.** In plain language — "split rent three ways," "stream $4,000 over 30 days," "send $50 to jordan.vel."
2. **Velfi understands it.** The AI agent reads your instruction, figures out what kind of payment you mean, and fills in the details (who, how much, when).
3. **You confirm.** Velfi shows you exactly what it's about to do. You approve with a single tap.
4. **It runs.** The payment executes on-chain in under a second. For recurring instructions, Velfi keeps it running automatically — you don't have to do anything again.

### The agent proposes, you approve

This is the most important thing to understand about Velfi: **the AI never moves money on its own.**

The agent's job is to translate your words into a payment and *propose* it. Nothing happens until you approve. You're always in control, and you always see exactly what will happen before it does.

{% hint style="info" %}
Think of Velfi like a really capable assistant. It does all the work of setting up the payment — but it always hands it to you to sign off before anything moves.
{% endhint %}

### Recurring instructions run on their own

Some instructions are one-time ("send $50 now"). Others are ongoing ("pay my designer every Friday"). For ongoing ones, you approve once, and Velfi handles every future payment automatically — no need to come back and re-do it each time.

### Where it all happens

Every payment settles on the Sui blockchain. That means it's fast (under a second), cheap (near-zero fees), and verifiable (recorded on-chain). But you never have to interact with any of that directly — Velfi handles it invisibly.
