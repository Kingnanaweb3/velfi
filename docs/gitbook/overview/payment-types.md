# Payment types

Velfi supports a range of payment types — all triggered by a plain-language instruction. Here's what you can do.

### Send

A simple, instant transfer to a `.vel` name or an email address.

> "Send $50 to jordan.vel."

[Full guide →](../guides/sending-money.md)

### Split

Divide one payment across many recipients, each with their own amount or percentage.

> "Split this 40/30/30 between three .vel names."

[Full guide →](../guides/splitting-payments.md)

### Stream

Pay someone continuously over a period of time — ideal for salaries, vesting, or contractor pay.

> "Stream $4,000 to alex.vel over 30 days."

[Full guide →](../guides/streaming-salaries.md)

### Schedule

Set up recurring payments on a cadence you choose.

> "Send rent to landlord.vel every Friday."

[Full guide →](../guides/automations.md)

### Escrow

Hold funds until a condition is met, then release them — and cancel anytime before release.

> "Hold $2,000 until the work is delivered, then release."

[Full guide →](../guides/escrow.md)

### Automate

Create rules that trigger on events, like saving a portion of every payment you receive.

> "Move 10% of every payment I receive into savings."

[Full guide →](../guides/automations.md)

### Send to an email

Send money to someone who isn't on Velfi yet. They get an invitation, sign up, and claim it.

> "Send $50 to jordan@gmail.com — they claim it when they sign up."

[Full guide →](../guides/email-to-claim.md)

***

All of these come from one place: **you describing what you want.** You don't need to remember which "type" to use — just say what you mean, and Velfi picks the right one.
