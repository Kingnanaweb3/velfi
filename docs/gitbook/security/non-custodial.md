# Non-custodial by design

Velfi never holds your money. This is one of the most important things to understand about how it works.

### What "non-custodial" means

A **custodial** service holds your funds for you — like a bank, or most crypto exchanges. You trust them to keep your money safe and give it back when you ask.

Velfi is **non-custodial**. Your money is controlled by you and only you. Velfi can build and propose payments, but it cannot move your funds — only your approval can.

### Why it matters

* **No one can freeze or seize your balance.** There's no company account holding your money.
* **Velfi can't lose your funds.** They're not in Velfi's possession to lose.
* **You're always in control.** Every payment requires your explicit approval.

### The AI can't spend your money

Velfi's AI agent reads your instructions and proposes payments — but it has no ability to move money on its own. It has no keys and no spending power. The worst an mistaken or misbehaving agent could do is suggest a payment, which you would simply reject.

{% hint style="success" %}
**The rule is simple:** the agent proposes, you approve, and only you hold the keys. Money never moves without you.
{% endhint %}

### Every payment is verifiable

Because payments settle on the Sui blockchain, every transaction is recorded publicly and can be independently verified. Your payment history doesn't depend on trusting Velfi's servers.
