# Sign-in & zkLogin

Velfi lets you sign in with Google and skip seed phrases entirely. The technology that makes this possible is called **zkLogin**.

### The problem with seed phrases

Most crypto wallets give you a long secret phrase you must store forever. It's a single point of failure:

* Lose it, and your money is gone permanently.
* Leak it, and someone can take everything.

For most people, this is the scariest and most error-prone part of using crypto.

### How zkLogin fixes it

zkLogin lets you create and access a secure wallet using an account you already have — your Google login — without ever generating a seed phrase.

It works using a **zero-knowledge proof**: a cryptographic method that lets Velfi confirm you're signed in with your Google account without exposing your private information or linking your identity to your wallet publicly.

### What this means for you

* **No seed phrase to write down or lose.**
* **Sign in on any device** with the same Google account and your wallet returns.
* **Still fully yours.** Signing in with Google is just how you unlock your wallet — Google doesn't control your funds, and neither does Velfi.

{% hint style="info" %}
zkLogin is a built-in feature of the Sui blockchain, designed specifically to make self-custody simple and safe for everyday people.
{% endhint %}

### Your keys, your control

Even though sign-in feels as easy as any normal app, you remain in full control of your money. zkLogin gives you the convenience of a familiar login with the security of self-custody.
