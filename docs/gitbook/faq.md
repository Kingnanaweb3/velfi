# FAQ

### Is Velfi custodial?

No. Velfi is non-custodial by design. Every payment is approved by you, and only your keys can move money. Velfi never takes custody of your funds. Read more in [Non-custodial by design](security/non-custodial.md).

### What does a .vel name cost?

Your `.vel` name is free to claim during early access. It replaces wallet addresses for you and anyone who pays you.

### Which blockchain does Velfi use?

Velfi is built on **Sui**, chosen for its sub-second finality, near-zero fees, and native sign-in technology. More networks may be supported over time, but Sui is the foundation today.

### Is my money safe?

Yes. Your funds are self-custodial, you sign in without a seed phrase via zkLogin, payments run as safe Move smart contracts, and every transaction is recorded on-chain and verifiable. See [Security](security/non-custodial.md).

### Do I need crypto experience?

Not at all. You sign in with Google, claim a name, and tell Velfi what you want in plain language. There are no wallets, seed phrases, or gas tokens to manage.

### Can the AI spend my money?

No. The AI agent only reads your instructions and proposes payments. It has no keys and cannot move funds on its own. Nothing happens until you approve it.

### What happens if I lose my phone?

Nothing is lost. Because your wallet comes from your Google sign-in, you simply sign in again on a new device and your wallet and balance return.

### Can I send money to someone who isn't on Velfi?

Yes. You can send to an email address, and the recipient claims the funds when they sign up. See [Send to an email](guides/email-to-claim.md).

### Can I cancel a payment?

One-time payments settle instantly and can't be reversed once approved, so check details before confirming. Escrow funds and unclaimed email payments can be reclaimed before they're released or claimed. Automations can be canceled anytime.

### When does Velfi launch?

You can join the waitlist now and claim your name. The app is coming soon — waitlist members get early access first.

### Does Velfi have a token?

No. Velfi has no token. It's a product for moving and managing money, not a cryptocurrency.
