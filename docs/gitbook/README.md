# Welcome to Velfi

**Velfi is an AI money agent.** You tell it what you want in plain language — "split rent three ways," "pay my designer every Friday," "hold this until the work ships" — and Velfi builds the payment and runs it. One sentence in, money moves.

No seed phrases. No gas tokens. No wallet addresses to copy. You sign in with Google, claim a name like `alice.vel`, and start instructing your money.

### What you can do

* **Send** money instantly to a `.vel` name — or to an email address the recipient claims later.
* **Split** a payment across many people, each with their own share.
* **Stream** a salary or payout continuously over time.
* **Schedule** recurring payments on any cadence.
* **Escrow** funds until a condition is met, and cancel anytime.
* **Automate** rules like saving a percentage of everything you receive.

### Built on Sui

Velfi runs on the Sui blockchain, so payments settle in under a second at near-zero cost. But you never have to think about any of that — the chain is invisible. Your money just does what you tell it to.

{% hint style="info" %}
**You always hold the keys.** Velfi is non-custodial. It builds and proposes your payments, but only you can approve them. Velfi never takes control of your funds.
{% endhint %}
