# What is Velfi?

Velfi is an app that lets you move and manage money by simply describing what you want. Instead of filling out forms, copying addresses, or setting up complicated transfers, you write a sentence — and Velfi handles the rest.

### The idea

Most money tasks are repetitive and rule-based. Salaries recur. Rent splits the same way every month. Savings should happen automatically. Velfi treats each of these as an instruction you can say in plain language, then turns it into a real payment that runs on its own.

You might tell Velfi:

> "Send $50 to jordan.vel."

> "Split this bill 40/30/30 between three people."

> "Stream $4,000 to my contractor over 30 days."

> "Move 10% of every payment I receive into savings."

Velfi reads the instruction, builds the right kind of payment, and asks you to confirm. Once you approve, it runs — and for recurring instructions, it keeps running automatically.

### What makes it different

* **You talk to it.** The interface is conversation, not forms.
* **It actually acts.** Other tools advise you on money. Velfi executes.
* **It's yours.** Velfi is non-custodial — only you can approve payments, and only you hold the keys.
* **It's effortless.** No seed phrases, no gas tokens, no addresses. Sign in with Google and go.

### Who it's for

Anyone who moves money with intent — paying a team, splitting costs with friends, sending money to family, automating savings, running subscriptions, or holding funds in escrow for a deal. If you can describe it, Velfi can do it.
