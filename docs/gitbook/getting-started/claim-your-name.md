# Claim your .vel name

Your `.vel` name is your identity on Velfi. It replaces the long, unreadable wallet addresses used elsewhere in crypto with something simple and memorable — like `alice.vel`.

### Why you need one

Instead of asking someone to send money to a string of 40+ random characters, you just share your name:

> "Send it to `alice.vel`."

Anyone paying you uses your name. You use theirs. It's like a username for your money.

### How to claim it

1. After signing in, you'll be prompted to choose a name.
2. Type the name you want (letters and numbers).
3. If it's available, claim it — it's yours.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Names are first-come, first-served.** Once someone claims a name, it's taken. If you want a specific name, claim it early.
{% endhint %}

### Tips for choosing

* Keep it short and easy to say out loud.
* Use something recognizable — your name, your brand, or your handle.
* This is how people will find and pay you, so pick something you're happy to share.

### Using your name

Once claimed, your `.vel` name works everywhere in Velfi:

* People send you money with it.
* You appear as your name in payments, splits, and streams.
* It's the anchor for your whole Velfi identity.
