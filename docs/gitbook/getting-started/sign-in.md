# Sign in

Getting into Velfi takes seconds. There's no wallet to install, no seed phrase to write down, and nothing to back up.

### How to sign in

1. Open Velfi.
2. Tap **Sign in with Google**.
3. Choose your Google account.

That's it. You now have a secure, self-custodial wallet — created automatically from your Google sign-in.

### Why there's no seed phrase

Traditional crypto wallets give you a 12- or 24-word "seed phrase" that you must store safely forever. Lose it and your money is gone; leak it and someone can steal everything.

Velfi removes that risk entirely. Your wallet is derived from your Google account using a privacy-preserving technology called **zkLogin**. There's no secret phrase to lose, write down, or have stolen.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Switch phones anytime.** Because your wallet comes from your Google sign-in, you just sign in again on a new device and the same wallet — and the same balance — comes right back.
{% endhint %}

### Is it really mine?

Yes. Even though you sign in with Google, Velfi is **non-custodial** — you hold the keys to your funds, not Velfi and not Google. Signing in with Google is just how you access your wallet; it doesn't give anyone else control over your money.
